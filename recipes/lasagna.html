Delicious Lasagna


Find it here on allrecipes.com

Lasagna, a quintessential Italian dish, is a harmonious symphony of flavors and textures. Layers of tender lasagna noodles interlaced with rich marinara sauce, creamy béchamel, and a savory blend of cheeses, typically mozzarella, ricotta, and Parmesan, create a mouthwatering masterpiece. Each bite is a delightful medley of hearty pasta, tangy tomato sauce, and indulgent cheese, offering a comforting embrace reminiscent of home-cooked meals shared with loved ones. Whether served as a cozy family dinner or a gourmet centerpiece for a special occasion, lasagna never fails to captivate with its comforting allure and timeless appeal.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • ¾ pound lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • ½ cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 ½ teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt, divided, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • ¾ pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
